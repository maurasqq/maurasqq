## about me

i’m a systems engineering student focused on turning raw business data into clear, validated, and decision-ready insights.

i enjoy working across the full analytics flow: querying data with sql, validating consistency between sources, shaping datasets with python, and building power bi dashboards centered on kpis, readability, and trust.

my strongest interest is not just visualizing information, but making sure the numbers behind it are accurate, reconciled, and useful for real business decisions.
